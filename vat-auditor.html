Workflow

How It Works.

01
Upload PDF Report
Simply upload your ZATCA VAT return PDF. The AI immediately begins analyzing the bilingual (Arabic & English) document structure, recognizing all fields automatically.
02
AI Extracts All Data Instantly
The AI engine extracts Company Name, 15-digit VAT ID, tax quarters, period dates, and all Sales/Purchases/Tax figures — populating the audit fields in seconds, not hours.
03
Review, Adjust & Reconcile
Professional auditors can manually input VAT credits carried forward, corrections, and journal adjustments. Compare with previous quarters using the Comparison Engine.
04
Generate Audit-Ready Report
Export a clean, professional summary suitable for internal records or ZATCA review. Save as draft, or print directly for compliance documentation.
