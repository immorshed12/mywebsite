Featured AI Project

Intelligence in Accounting.

KSA VAT Auditor AI

An AI-powered web application built specifically on ZATCA regulations. It analyzes VAT return data, automates compliance checks, and identifies potential risks — reducing manual review time by 60%+.

🤖

AI Extraction Engine — Parses bilingual (Arabic & English) ZATCA VAT return PDFs, auto-extracts Company Name, 15-digit VAT ID, tax quarters, and all table data.

📊

Full VAT Breakdown — Replicates official ZATCA Tables 1–12: Sales, Purchases, manual journal entries, net VAT balance calculations in real-time.

⚖️

Comparison Engine — Side-by-side period comparison with color-coded variance tracking to instantly identify compliance risks.

💾

Draft Management — Save, rename, and export reports as JSON. Local persistence for multiple audit drafts.
